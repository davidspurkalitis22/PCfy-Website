Our Story

PCFY was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate PC enthusiasts in Galway with a simple mission: to provide high-quality custom PC builds, reliable repair services, and premium components at fair prices.

What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted name in the local PC community. We pride ourselves on our technical expertise, personalized service, and commitment to helping our customers find the perfect PC solutions for their needs.

Whether you're a gamer looking for the ultimate gaming rig, a professional needing a powerful workstation, or someone who just needs their computer fixed, we're here to help with honest advice and quality service.
